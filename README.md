# ☕ Bean & Bloom


🌐 Live Demo : https://bloom-your-table.lovable.app

---

## 📌 Project Overview

**Bean & Bloom** is a modern and responsive cafe website designed to help a local cafe establish a strong online presence, showcase its menu and ambience, attract new customers, and encourage table reservations.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Many local cafes rely only on walk-in customers and social media pages, making it difficult for new customers to discover their services and menu.

### Challenges:

* No professional online presence
* Limited customer engagement
* Difficulty showcasing menu and ambience
* No easy way for customers to contact or reserve tables

---

## 💡 Solution

Bean & Bloom provides a modern business website that allows customers to:

* Explore the cafe and its story
* View menu offerings
* Browse the gallery
* Reserve tables
* Contact the cafe easily
* Locate the cafe through maps and contact details
